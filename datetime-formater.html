Simply utility to play with date time formats and see the results in multiple cultures. You only have to type a date format and you will se how should be written in diffrent languages an cultures.

The dates are build using MomentJs, one of the most helpfull libraries to work with dates and times in javascript world.

For the freaks and nostalgics gamers I styled the site with ness.css.
